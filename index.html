About Flujee



Flujee is a global foodie network meant to
bring restaurants and people closer by
giving both parties an equal platform.

But most importantly, we are focusing on
the experience of dining out and turning it
into a unique and fun experience.

Apps nowadays do not take food allergies
into consideration, also explaining your
food intolerance can be tedious and
confusing, especially abroad.

You could use flujee to set up an accurate
representation of your food preferences so
that you can essentially communicate with
other users and restaurants in a more
faster, global and convenient manner.








The allergy feature should be used as a helpful guide for yourself and others to determine the most satisfactory service and is not meant to be a replacement of any food intolerance medication. We strongly advise In seeking the necessary testing of food allergies by frequent visits to your doctor.
